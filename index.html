About –

I'm a product designer with experience in all phases of the product design lifecycle, from eliciting business and customer goals, to exploratory research, championing a product vision, and bringing that product to life through thoughtful design. I'm also an experienced facilitator, teaching and leading teams through the user-centered design process.

I've had user-centered research and design roles at Google, Dick's Sporting Goods, GlaxoSmithKline, and Penn State's College of Information & Technology. My work has touched billions of users.

When I'm not designing, you'll find me woodworking, recording or performing music, or spending time with my family.
